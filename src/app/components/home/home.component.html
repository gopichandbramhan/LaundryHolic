How we work ?

1

Customer will signup to the Laundryholic application and will be able to create order for their garments any time.

2

On the next step, customer will select a laundry which best fits their budget, which gives them the best experience of laundry service.

3

After the order is confirmed, delivery boy will collect the clothes from the customer's location and give them to the laundry store

4

The garments collected from the customer will undergo a thorough dry cleaning process using industry leading equipments and solvents

5

Once the garments are washed, it will be send for folding and packaging process. Now the freshly dry cleaned garments are carefully folded and packed.

6

The order after packaging will be delivered to the customer's location within the given period of time.
